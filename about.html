Research

I have three projects on the go right now:

Currently, I'm working on a URI grant to study the theoretical infrastructure for judicial decisions in antitrust law. I'm working under the supervision of Dr. Claudia Landeo on this.

At the same time, I've also received a NSERC USRA to work on research in mathematical economics. We're studying the Prisoner's Dilemma in continuous time with noisy signals. This project is being complete under the supervision of Dr. Chris Frei.

Lastly, I'm working on a project that studies how countries can work to develop robust tax bases. I'm working on this one by myself. It's still in the very early stages of its life, but I have high hopes for it.
